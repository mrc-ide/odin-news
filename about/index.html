About

This news site covers the researcher-facing part of the odin stack, covering:

  • odin, our DSL for dynamical models
  • dust, a high-performance parallel simulation engine
  • mode, a parallel ODE engine, soon to be merged into dust
  • odin.dust, transpilation support for odin models to dust and mode
  • mcstate, statistical support for dust and mode models, implementing particle filtering, particle MCMC, and other algorithms.

This stack was built by us at RESIDE to support research activities within the MRC Centre for Global Infectious Disease Analysis.
